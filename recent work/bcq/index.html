WHO IS BCQ

BCQ Consulting BCQ provides its services to meet the needs of all public and private sectors to develop their operations, services and products, and to help them compete effectively in the best services and raise the regional and global economy. BCQ Consulting seeks to work with the public and private sectors as a partner in the success, development, continuous improvement and performance in anticipation of the application of international quality systems according to specifications and qualifying the bodies and granting them international quality certificates. BCQ Consulting BCQ is one of the most important conformity and grant assessment agencies in all Gulf countries and Arab countries. The company is the exclusive agent of the LSA Certificates (Limited Liability Limited) (STAUNCHLY) (Care CPL) and contracted with Gcerti (QA) and accredited by the GCC countries and accredited by the DAC and the American Accreditation Council (IAS) ) And the international accreditation (IAF). BCQ Consulting is engaged in the development and development of the public and private sector and improving the quality of its performance through training, development, continuous improvement and finding the best administrative methods that help to raise their service and productivity capabilities.
